Why You Need This

Without AI & IoT
  • Relying on manual inspections of safety gear and machinery health.
  • Not knowing equipment is failing until it breaks, halting operations.
  • Manually counting inventory boxes and shipments in hot warehouses.
  • Security cameras are passive recorders that only show footage after theft.
With Axolv Smart IoT
  • Smart cameras automatically check safety gear compliance in real-time.
  • Telemetry sensors forecast failure states days before they occur.
  • Real-time vision models track inventory and package counts.
  • Active camera grids trigger immediate alerts when anomalies occur.

What It Actually Does

1. Automated Safety Compliance

Scenario: Workers entering a high-risk warehouse zone must wear hard hats.
Result: The smart camera feed monitors the entryway, detects if a worker is missing a helmet, and sounds a gentle alert instantly.

2. Predictive Failure Alerting

Scenario: A critical motor starts vibrating abnormally, indicating friction.
Result: Vibration sensors pick up the telemetry anomaly, flag it, and schedule maintenance before a breakdown occurs.

3. Visual Inventory Count

Scenario: Shipping boxes need to be counted as they flow past on conveyor belts.
Result: We deploy a vision classifier on your camera grid to track, count, and log inventory directly to your sales spreadsheet.
