FAQ

Frequently Asked Questions

Clear insights on how AI automation integrates into your business flow.

What is AI automation and how can it help my business? +
AI automation uses artificial intelligence to handle repetitive tasks, make data-driven decisions, and streamline business operations. It helps businesses reduce manual work by up to 80%, cut operational costs, minimize errors, and free up teams to focus on high-value strategic work. At Axolv, we build custom AI agents and workflow automation systems tailored to your specific business needs.
How much does AI automation cost for a small business? +
AI automation costs vary based on complexity and scope. Simple workflow automations can start from a few hundred dollars, while comprehensive AI agent systems with custom integrations are priced based on business requirements. Axolv offers a free 30-minute consultation to assess your needs and provide a tailored quote. Most businesses see ROI within 2-3 months through reduced manual labor and increased efficiency.
What types of business processes can Axolv automate? +
Axolv automates a wide range of business processes including customer support (AI chatbots and agents), lead generation and qualification, email marketing workflows, data entry and processing, invoice and billing automation, employee onboarding, social media management, reporting and analytics, CRM updates, and inventory management. If a process is repetitive and rule-based, it can likely be automated.
Do I need technical knowledge to use AI automation? +
No. Axolv specifically designs AI automation solutions for non-technical business owners. We handle all the technical implementation, integration, and maintenance. You get simple dashboards and interfaces to monitor your automated workflows. Our team provides full onboarding and ongoing support to ensure you get maximum value from your AI systems.
How long does it take to implement AI automation? +
Implementation timelines vary by project scope. Simple workflow automations can be deployed within 1-2 weeks. More complex AI agent systems with multiple integrations typically take 4-8 weeks. Axolv follows an agile approach — we deliver working automation modules incrementally so you start seeing value immediately, even before the full system is complete.
What is the difference between AI agents and traditional automation? +
Traditional automation follows fixed rules and scripts (if-this-then-that). AI agents, on the other hand, use artificial intelligence to understand context, make decisions, learn from data, and handle complex scenarios that rule-based systems cannot. AI agents can process natural language, adapt to new situations, and perform tasks that previously required human judgment — making them far more powerful and flexible than traditional automation.
